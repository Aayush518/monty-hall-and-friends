What is the Monty Hall Problem?

The Monty Hall problem is a famous probability puzzle based on a game show scenario:

  1. There are three doors. Behind one door is a car, and behind the others are goats.
  2. You pick a door, but it remains closed.
  3. The host, who knows what's behind the doors, opens another door, revealing a goat.
  4. The host then asks if you want to switch your choice to the remaining unopened door or stick with your original choice.

Should you switch or stay with your original choice?
